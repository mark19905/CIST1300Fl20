Services

Each experience begins with a thorough consultation, followed by a tailored experience.
Our salon is departmentalized, meaning we have color specialists and style specialists. When booking online, if you wish to receive both color and cut, please book accordingly.

Our color services do not include a blow dry. We have fully-stocked styling stations for you to use to blow dry your own hair. *this is not available currently due to Covid-19* If you would like a full professional blow dry following your color service, please request this at the time of booking. An additional charge will be added for this service.
